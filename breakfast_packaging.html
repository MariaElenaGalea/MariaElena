The brief was to choose a food cluster from the list provided and create a packaging for each item. A target market is to be selected and researched. The products created should fit the needs of the researched target audience.

Product Specifications

  • luxurious
  • practical (ready-to-go)
  • energy boosting
  • environmental friendly


Sarah Borg is usually very busy at work, she wakes up early and does not have time to prepare breakfast. She requested a product design that she could keep at her office. The breakfast packaging needed to be reusable, original and with a minimalistic design that does not resemble the usual cereal and other breakfast packaging design.
