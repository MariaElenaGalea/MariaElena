photojournalism

A documentation of Portelli’s confectionery business.

The shop is located in Victoria; well-known for the typical narrow streets, character houses and the famous Ċittadella.

My intention was to portray the whole story while focusing on their behind the scenes; that is, baking. This is because it is one of our traditions as Maltese, and also because of the fact that in the modern world of today many, especially tourists might not know about these traditional practices.
